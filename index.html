Lato

Lato is a sans serif typeface family started in the summer of 2010 by Warsaw-based designer Lukasz Dziedzic ("Lato" means "Summer" in Polish). In December 2010 the Lato family was published undr the Open Font License by his foundry tyPoland, with support from Google.
